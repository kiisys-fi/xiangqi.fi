General websites

  • World Xiangqi Federation website - Official website of the World Xiangqi Federation (WXF). It contains information on upcoming official tournaments, a list of xiangqi associations and federated players and the official xiangqi tournament rules.
  • Xqinenglish - Wide collection of articles on various aspects of the game, featuring embedded interactive boards to visualize the discussed concepts.

Books

Introductory books

  • Xiangqi For Absolute Beginners: Teach Yourself XIANGQI step by step - General overview of the game and its phases.
  • Understanding the elephant series
    • Part 1 - History of xiangqi.
    • Part 2 - Basic rules and mating techniques.
    • Part 3a - Introduction to endgames.
    • Part 3b - Introduction to endgames.
  • H.T.Lau: Chinese Chess: An Introduction to China's Ancient Game of Strategy - General overview of the game and its phases. It discusses rules, basic openings and endgames as well as some middle game strategies and tactics.
  • XIANGQI Introduction to Chinese Chess for beginners - Introductory book by grandmaster Ye Lin, discussing the game rules and strategies as well as its history.
  • Lexicon of xiangqi - English terms related to xiangqi with Chinese translation, over 1600 entries. This is very useful to be able to read books in Chinese on the topic. A shorter free extract of the book is available here.

Opening

  • Chinese Chess: An Introduction to the Openings
  • Xiangqi opening trilogy by Master Yan Wenqing and Grandmaster Zhang Qiang - Considered one of the most complete and authoritative resource on xiangqi opening.
    • 象棋布局定式与战理 (Xiangqi opening tabiat and their principles) - Systematic overview of xiangqi openings. Some articles from chapters of the book available here and some videos discussing the book available here.
    • 布局骗着与对策 (Deceptions and countermeasures in xiangqi openings) - Typical traps in xiangqi opening and how not to fall for them. Video discussions of the book here. A partial English translation by WXF available here.
    • 布局疑形与攻击 (Dubious positions and how to attack them) - Typical opening mistakes and how to punish them. Video discussions available here.
  • Xiangqi: The basic of same direction cannon openings
  • Xiangqi D00-D29 Same Direction Cannons: Modern Xiangqi Opening Repertoire
  • 胡, 荣华. 夹炮屏风 反宫马布局研究 (Hu Ronghua's sandwiched horse defense treatise) - A video discussion of the book is available here.

Tactics / puzzles

  • Xiangqi basic tactics - Free ebook by WXF about xiangqi tactis.
  • Basic xiangqi chekmate methods - Free ebook by WXF about common checkmate techniques.
  • Checkmate Methods in Xiangqi Mid-Games - Free ebook by WXF about checkmate tactics frequently occurring during middle game.
  • Become a xiangqi assassin 1 - Exercise book with several puzzles with discussed solutions in increasing level of difficulty.
  • Become a xiangqi assassin 2 - Exercise book several puzzles with discussed solutions in increasing level of difficulty.

Ancient manuals

  • Plum Flower Manual by Wang - Ancient manual from the Qing dynasty, notorious for being one of the first introducing the screen-horse defense against central cannon.
  • Plum Flower Springs Manual - Ancient manual from the Qing dynasty, discussing in details several opening systems. It is also one of the few manuals discussing handicap games.
  • Unparalleled and Chong Ben Tang Plum Flower Manuals - Ancient manual from the Qing dynasty, which presents puzzles and discusses the foundations of the screen-horse defense.
  • One Hundred Variations in Xiangqi & Marvelous Variations Xiangqi Manual - Collection of endgame compositions and puzzles from the Ming dynasty.
  • Dreams of Divine Positions - One of the oldest collection of puzzles and endgame compositions.
  • Lan Ke Shen Ji - Collection of relatively straightforward endgame compositions, suited particularly to train calculation skills.
  • Secret in the Tangerine, volume 1, volume 2, volume 3, volume 4 - One of the oldest ancient manuals, considered a fundamental classic on the game.
  • Elegant pastime manual, volumes 1 & 2, volumes 3 & 4, volumes 5 & 6 - Collection of puzzles and endgame compositions from the Ming dynasty. This book is also rich of Chinese culture, with each composition name being a verse from famous Chinese poems.
  • Secret record of xiangqi - One of the first books from the Qing dynasty, popular for presenting more complex endgame problems compared to its predecessors.
  • The xiangqi master from the cave - Ancient manual from the Song dynasty, presenting several games commentaries with detailed explanations.

Engines

  • Pikafish - Strong engine for xiangqi based on stockfish.
  • Fairy-stockfish - Strong engine based on stockfish, which also supports other chess and xiangqi variants.

Computer xiangqi

  • Chinese chess encyclopedia - Overview of some computer xiangqi concepts and description of ECCO opening classification.
  • Chinese Chess File Format - Description of the WXF format by WXF.
  • Computer Chinese Chess - Overview of the field. A bit old, but still relevant for the basic concepts.
  • Tsan-sheng Hsu page - Lecture notes and several research papers on computer xiangqi.
  • Xiangqi chess engine in JavaScript - Video tutorials on developing a simple xiangqi engine from scratch in JavaScript.
  • A completed implementation for xiangqi rules - Paper describing an algorithm to check for perpetual check and perpetual chasing.

Other useful tools

  • xiangqi-setup - Python script to generate board images from FEN or WXF formats.
  • xiangqi-book-example - LaTeX template to write text about xiangqi, using xiangqi-setup to generate board images from FEN notation.
  • 棋弈江湖 (Xiangqi PWA) - Full-featured xiangqi platform with the world's first open-source board recognition system. Includes complete ONNX models for chess piece detection and classification, AI analysis powered by Pikafish, 7300+ puzzle database, and professional game review. All models are MIT licensed.
